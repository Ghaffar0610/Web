Our Journey

How a passion for exploration became a mission to share the world's wonders

From Backpacker to Travel Pioneer

Founded in 2010 by Umer Ahmed, Travel with Umer began as a dream to make authentic travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started as a solo backpacking adventure across Southeast Asia evolved into a mission to connect people with transformative journeys.

Umer's philosophy was simple: travel should be about more than just sightseeing. It should challenge perspectives, foster connections, and create memories that last a lifetime. This belief became the foundation of our company.

Today, we've grown from a one-person operation to a team of passionate travel experts, but our core values remain unchanged. We still personally vet every destination, work directly with local communities, and design itineraries that go beyond the tourist trail.
